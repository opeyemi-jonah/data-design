USE CASE

Use Case: Access bet9ja.com

Actor: Abayo

Goal: Place bets

OVERVIEW: Abayo accesses the betting website and navigates to his favourite soccer league,check the news injury update, game history and pick his predicted games and places bets. He checks his inbox for new messages.

    Abayo's action:
  • Abayo logs in to bet9ja.com
  • Abayomi selects his favorite sport
  • Abayo selects one of his favorite soccer league
  • Abayo selects the games he wants to bet on
  • He is provided with different bet types(win,lose,draw, hadicap,half time win, etc)
  • He navgates to his inbox chat
  • Receives messages from friend
  • Replies messages
  • Navigates to sport news
  • Filter news to soccer injury reports/update
  • Checks for the games he chooses rival history and statistics
    System response:
  • Display homepage with navigation bars of top 5 world sports
  • Display soccer leagues(Abayo's favorite league and most non-favorite selected by Abayo in the past)
  • Display bet options
  • Save bet option(s) selection in a cart
  • Display Abayo's account inbox page
  • Display response from other user account if available
  • Send text to other user account
  • Display latest news on injury of soccer players
  • Display bar chart of game rival history and statistic data from ESPN API
    Usage Preconditions:
  • Abayo creates an account
  • Abayo links his bank account to his user account
  • Abayo makes a deposit via direct deposit or wire deposit
  • Abayo places bets and stakes on them.
    Usage Post-conditions:
  • Abayo places stakes on bets made
  • Abayo gets bet results at end of games selected
  • Abayo withdraws winnings to bank account

Frequency of Use: Abayo accesses the webpage on a daily basis.
