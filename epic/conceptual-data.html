Entities & Attributes

    STUDENT:
  • StudentID (primary key)
  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Address
  • CGPA
  • FacultyID
    COURSES:
  • CourseID (primary key)
  • Course title
    LECTURER:
  • LecturerID (primary key)
  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Rank
    FACULTY:
  • FacultyID (primary key)
  • LectureID (foreign key)
  • StudentID (foreign key)
  • CourseID (foreign key)

Relations

  • One student can take n courses (1 to n)
  • one Faculty contains m students, n lecturers and p courses (1 to m),(1 to n),(1 to p)
